About PGPC

Padre Garcia Polytechnic College (PGPC) is a premiere higher learning institution located in Padre Garcia, Batangas. It is committed to providing quality and accessible education to every young Garciano who dreams of a better future. More than just a school, PGPC is the result of the deep and genuine desire of Hon. Mike Rivera to give the youth of Padre Garcia the opportunity to study in an affordable, reliable, and high-quality college within their own hometown.
